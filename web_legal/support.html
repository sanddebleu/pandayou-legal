Common questions

  • Reminders not firing (iPhone) — iPhone Settings → PandaYou → Notifications: allow Alerts. Check Focus / Do Not Disturb. Open PandaYou → Settings → Reminders not firing?
  • Sign-in issues — Use the email you registered with, or try Google sign-in in Safari.
  • Panda+ / billing — Manage subscriptions in the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android). In-app: Settings → Panda+.
  • Delete account — Settings → Login & profile → Delete account, or see our delete-account page.
