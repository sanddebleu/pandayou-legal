PandaYou — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

PandaYou is a personal habit, diary, gratitude and focus app for iPhone and Android. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can delete it.

Sign-in required

PandaYou requires sign-in — with Google or email and password. Your habits, diary, mood log, and other content are stored on your device while you use the app.

Optional Panda+ subscription (via the App Store on iPhone or Google Play on Android) adds automatic encrypted cloud backup and restore across reinstalls and devices. Free users can export their data manually from Settings → Data.

Vent to panda & AI features

PandaYou uses cloud AI (Google Gemini, via our servers) for Vent to panda, Plan my day, smart voice reminders, stuck-habit coach, morning check-in, mood quotes, journal scan, evening reflection, and similar features.

Content you send is processed only to produce a response for you. We do not use it for advertising or sell it. AI replies are wellness support only — not therapy or medical advice. If you are in crisis, use Crisis help inside vent or contact local emergency services.

New accounts get a 7-day AI trial with usage caps. After the trial, AI features require optional Panda+.

Friends, nudges & co-habits

If you pair with a friend, we store a link between accounts and a progress snapshot you choose to publish (not diary text). Paired friends can send live push nudges via Firebase Cloud Messaging. We keep only a daily send count per pair for rate limiting — not copies of nudge messages on our servers.

What we collect

  • Account: email and Google display name (passwords handled by Supabase Auth).
  • Your content: habits, diary, moods, photos — on device; with Panda+, encrypted cloud backup.
  • Push token: per device for friend nudges and optional reminders.
  • Microphone: only while you record voice or dictate.

We do not sell your data, show third-party ads, or collect location, contacts, SMS, or call logs.

Who we share it with

  • Supabase — account, optional Panda+ backup, friend links, push tokens.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — push delivery.
  • Google Gemini — AI text and journal scan images.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play — process Panda+ subscription payments; we receive purchase status, not card details.

When you tap Share or Invite, content goes to your phone’s share sheet — you choose who receives it.

Deleting your account and data

  • In the app: Settings → Login & profile → Delete account.
  • Without the app: delete-account page or email PandaYou.app@gmail.com from your account email.

Your rights

Export data in-app (Settings → Data). EU/UK users have GDPR rights — email PandaYou.app@gmail.com; we respond within 30 days.

Children

PandaYou is intended for users aged 13 and over.
