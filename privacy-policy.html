PandaYou — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

PandaYou is a personal habit, diary, gratitude and focus app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can delete it.

Sign-in required

PandaYou requires Google sign-in to use the app. Your habits, diary, mood log, and other content are stored in an encrypted cloud backup tied to your account. We do not offer a separate anonymous or offline-only mode in the public app.

Vent to panda & AI features

PandaYou uses cloud AI (Google Gemini, via our servers) to generate short text when you use features such as Vent to panda, Plan my day, smart voice reminders, the stuck-habit coach, morning check-in follow-ups, mood and morning quotes, journal mood reads, evening reflection summaries, journal photo scan (handwriting → text), and Letters from You drafts.

When you use these features, the text or image you send is transmitted to our servers and processed only to produce a response for you. We do not use this content for advertising or sell it. AI replies are wellness support only — not therapy, medical advice, or emergency services. If you are in crisis, contact local emergency services (e.g. 988 in the US).

If an AI reply concerns you, report it via Settings → Send feedback. Voice input is converted to text on your device; we do not store raw vent audio. Weekly usage caps apply to most AI features.

Friends, nudges & co-habits

If you pair with a friend, we store a link between accounts and a progress snapshot you choose to publish (display name and habit stats — not diary text). Paired friends can send live push nudges via Firebase Cloud Messaging. We keep only a daily send count per pair for rate limiting — not copies of nudge messages.

What we collect

  • Account (required): Google email and display name.
  • Your content: habits, diary, gratitude, moods, letters, memory jar, photos you attach, and XP — in an encrypted cloud backup.
  • Push token: Firebase Cloud Messaging token per device.
  • Friend data: pairing links, snapshots you publish, and nudge rate-limit counters.
  • Microphone: only while you actively record a voice memo, dictate in Plan my day, or dictate a vent message.
  • Journal scan photos: sent to our servers for AI transcription; not kept as separate files after processing.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs stored locally on your device only (not uploaded automatically).

We do not sell your data, show third-party ads, or collect location, contacts, SMS, or call logs.

Who we share it with

We use service providers to run PandaYou. They process data only on our instructions — not for their own advertising or resale.

  • Supabase — account, encrypted backup, private media storage, friend links, push tokens.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — push delivery (device token + notification title/body).
  • Google Gemini — AI text and journal scan images, solely to generate replies.

Data is encrypted in transit. We do not sell data or share it with advertisers or data brokers.

How long we keep it

As long as your account is active, until you delete it.

Deleting your account and data

  • In the app: Settings → Login & profile → Delete account (immediate, irreversible).
  • Without the app: delete-account page or email PandaYou.app@gmail.com from your Google account email.

Your rights

Export data in-app (Settings → Backup → Export). Request access, correction, or deletion via PandaYou.app@gmail.com. EU/UK users have GDPR rights; we respond within 30 days.

Children

PandaYou is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone younger.

Changes

Material changes will be reflected on this page and in the in-app policy with an updated date.
