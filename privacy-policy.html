PandaYou — Privacy Policy

Effective: 18 May 2026

PandaYou is a personal habit, diary, gratitude and focus app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can delete it. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the features you use.

What we collect

  • Account: your email address (and, if you sign in with Google, your Google display name) — used only to authenticate you and sync your data across devices.
  • Your content: habit names, reminder times, diary and gratitude entries, mood entries, and any photos you attach. Stored so we can back them up and restore them when you reinstall or switch devices.
  • Push token: a Firebase Cloud Messaging token per device, so reminders and friend nudges can reach you.
  • Microphone: only while you are actively recording a voice diary memo, using voice input in Plan my day, or dictating a vent message. Audio is transcribed for that session; we do not record or listen in the background.
  • AI features (Vent to panda, Plan my day, smart reminders, stuck-habit coach): when you use these while signed in, the text you send is transmitted to our servers and processed by Google Gemini to generate a reply. We use this only to run the feature — not for ads or profiling. Vent chats are not reviewed by humans unless you contact support.

We do not sell your data, show third-party ads, or use advertising trackers. We do not collect your location, contacts, SMS, or call logs.

How it is used and shared

Your content is stored on our backend provider (Supabase) and push is delivered via Google Firebase Cloud Messaging. AI requests are processed via our servers and Google Gemini. These providers process data only to deliver the app's features. Data is encrypted in transit.

Wellness & AI disclaimer

Vent to panda and related AI tools provide automated wellness support only. They are not therapy, medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or an emergency service. AI replies may be inaccurate. If you are in crisis, contact local emergency services or a crisis line (e.g. 988 in the US).

How long we keep it

As long as your account is active. If you stop using the app, your data remains so it can be restored if you return — until you delete it.

Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account and all cloud data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Login & profile → Delete account. This immediately and irreversibly removes your account, backups, reminders, and friend connections.
  • Without the app: visit https://sanddebleu.github.io/pandayou-legal/delete-account.html and follow the steps, or email PandaYou.app@gmail.com from your account email. We delete everything within 30 days (usually immediately).

Habit or diary data kept only on your device (if you never signed in) is removed when you uninstall the app.

Your rights

You can access, export, correct, or delete your data. Contact PandaYou.app@gmail.com for any request.

Children

PandaYou is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age required by local law.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update this page and the effective date above.
