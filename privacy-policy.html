PandaYou — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

PandaYou is a personal habit, diary, gratitude and focus app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can delete it. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the features you use.

Vent to panda & AI features

PandaYou uses cloud AI (Google Gemini, via our servers) to generate short text when you use features such as Vent to panda, Plan my day, smart voice reminders, the stuck-habit coach, morning check-in follow-ups, mood and morning quotes, journal mood reads, evening reflection summaries, journal photo scan (handwriting → text), and Letters from You drafts.

When you use these while signed in, the text or image you send is transmitted to our servers and processed only to produce a response for you. We do not use this content for advertising or sell it. AI replies are wellness support only — not therapy, medical advice, or emergency services. If you are in crisis, contact local emergency services (e.g. 988 in the US).

Voice input is converted to text on your device; we do not store raw vent audio. Weekly usage caps apply to most AI features.

Friends, nudges & co-habits

If you pair with a friend, we store a link between accounts and a progress snapshot you choose to publish (display name and habit stats — not diary text). Paired friends can send live push nudges; notification title and short message travel via Firebase Cloud Messaging. We keep only a daily send count per pair for rate limiting — not copies of nudge messages.

Co-habit pacts live in your encrypted backup. You can remove friends or stop sharing anytime.

What we collect

  • Account: email and Google display name (Google sign-in only).
  • Your content: habits, diary, gratitude, moods, letters, memory jar, photos you attach, and XP — in an encrypted cloud backup when signed in, or only on-device if you never sign in.
  • Push token: Firebase Cloud Messaging token per device for friend nudges, co-habit reminders, and optional come-back pings.
  • Friend data: pairing links, snapshots you publish, and nudge rate-limit counters.
  • Microphone: only while you actively record a voice memo, dictate in Plan my day, or dictate a vent message. Not recorded in the background.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs stored locally on your device only (not uploaded automatically).

We do not sell your data, show third-party ads, or collect location, contacts, SMS, or call logs.

How it is used and shared

Your content is stored on Supabase. Push is delivered via Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google). AI requests are processed via our servers and Google Gemini. These providers process data only to deliver app features. Data is encrypted in transit.

How long we keep it

As long as your account is active, until you delete it. Local-only data is removed when you uninstall the app.

Deleting your account and data

  • In the app: Settings → Login & profile → Delete account (immediate, irreversible).
  • Without the app: delete-account page or email PandaYou.app@gmail.com from your account email.

Your rights

Export data in-app (Settings → Backup → Export). Request access, correction, or deletion via PandaYou.app@gmail.com. EU/UK users have GDPR rights; we respond within 30 days.

Children

PandaYou is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone younger.

Changes

Material changes will be reflected on this page and in the in-app policy with an updated date.
