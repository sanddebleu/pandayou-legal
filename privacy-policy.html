PandaYou — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

PandaYou is a personal habit, diary, gratitude and focus app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can delete it.

Sign-in required

PandaYou requires sign-in to use the app — with Google or email and password. Your habits, diary, mood log, and other content are stored on your device while you use the app. We do not offer a separate anonymous or offline-only mode in the public app.

Optional Panda+ subscription (via Google Play) adds automatic encrypted cloud backup and restore across reinstalls and devices. Free users can export their data manually from Settings → Data.

Vent to panda & AI features

PandaYou uses cloud AI (Google Gemini, via our servers) to generate short text when you use features such as Vent to panda, Plan my day, smart voice reminders, the stuck-habit coach, morning check-in follow-ups, mood and morning quotes, journal mood reads, evening reflection summaries, journal photo scan (handwriting → text), and Letters from You drafts.

When you use these features, the text or image you send is transmitted to our servers and processed only to produce a response for you. We do not use this content for advertising or sell it. AI replies can be wrong or incomplete — wellness support only, not therapy, medical advice, diagnosis, or emergency care. If you are in crisis, use Crisis help inside vent or contact local emergency services (e.g. 988 in the US).

If an AI reply concerns you, report it via Settings → Send feedback. Voice input in vent or Plan my day is converted to text on your device (or via the system speech service); we do not store raw vent audio.

New accounts get a one-time 7-day AI trial with weekly usage caps. After the trial, AI features require an optional Panda+ subscription. See Settings → Panda AI usage for current limits.

Friends, nudges & co-habits

If you pair with a friend (Panda ID or invite link), we store a link between accounts and a progress snapshot you choose to publish (display name and habit stats — not diary text). Paired friends can send live push nudges via Firebase Cloud Messaging. We keep only a daily send count per pair for rate limiting — not copies of nudge messages on our servers.

Co-habit pacts let two people track the same habit goal together. You can remove a friend or stop sharing at any time from the Friends screen.

What we collect

  • Account (required): email address and, for Google sign-in, your Google display name. Passwords are handled by Supabase Auth — we never store or see your password in plain text.
  • Your content: habits, diary, gratitude, moods, letters, memory jar, photos you attach, and XP — on your device; with Panda+, also in an encrypted cloud backup tied to your account.
  • Push token: Firebase Cloud Messaging token per device (for friend nudges and optional come-back pings if you opt in).
  • Friend data: pairing links, snapshots you publish, and nudge rate-limit counters.
  • Microphone: only while you actively record a voice memo, dictate in Plan my day, or dictate a vent message.
  • Journal scan photos: sent to our servers for AI transcription; not kept as separate files after processing.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs stored locally on your device only (not uploaded automatically).

We do not sell your data, show third-party ads, or collect location, contacts, SMS, or call logs.

Who we share it with

We use service providers to run PandaYou. They process data only on our instructions — not for their own advertising or resale.

  • Supabase — account (Google or email sign-in), optional Panda+ encrypted backup, private media storage, friend links, push tokens.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — push delivery (device token + notification title/body).
  • Google Gemini — AI text and journal scan images, solely to generate replies.
  • Google Play — processes Panda+ subscription payments; we receive purchase status, not your card details.

When you tap Share or Invite, PandaYou hands content to your phone’s system share sheet — you choose who receives it. We never auto-post or upload it.

Data is encrypted in transit. We do not sell data or share it with advertisers or data brokers.

How long we keep it

As long as your account is active, until you delete it. If you sign out and don’t return, your account and any Panda+ cloud data remain until you delete the account or your Panda+ backup lapses per our retention rules above.

Deleting your account and data

  • In the app: Settings → Login & profile → Delete account (immediate, irreversible).
  • Without the app: delete-account page or email PandaYou.app@gmail.com from the email address of the account you used to sign in.

Your rights

Export data in-app (Settings → Data → Backup habits or Full device backup). If you have Panda+ cloud backup, you can also export from Settings → Data → Cloud backup. Request access, correction, or deletion via PandaYou.app@gmail.com. EU/UK users have GDPR rights; we respond within 30 days.

Children

PandaYou is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone younger.

Changes

Material changes will be reflected on this page and in the in-app policy with an updated date.
