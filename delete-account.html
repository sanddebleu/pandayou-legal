Delete your PandaYou account & data

You can permanently delete your PandaYou account and all associated data. This is irreversible.

Fastest: delete in the app

  1. Open PandaYou.
  2. Go to Settings → Login & profile.
  3. Tap Delete account, type DELETE to confirm.

Your account and cloud data are removed immediately.

Don't have the app? Request deletion here

Email PandaYou.app@gmail.com from the email address of the account you want deleted, with the subject "Delete my PandaYou account". We verify ownership via that email, then delete the account.

What gets deleted

Everything tied to your account is permanently erased: your account and login, cloud backups of habits/diary/gratitude/mood, photos you attached, reminder schedules, push tokens, and friend connections.

Retention: deletion is immediate when done in-app. Email requests are completed within 30 days (typically sooner). We do not retain a copy afterward, except where law requires.
